Good and Bad Design Examples

Good Design

Dior

The design of this website is very clean and the embedded videos change based on the season and current product releases, but they are always very entertaining and exciting. The links to different categories and more information are easy to find on the header and the minimal design helps the videos not be overwhelming.

Associated Press

Many news websites are not very clean/organized, which was something that definitely annoyed me when I was taking a Journalism class in Fall 2024, but the Associated Press website is very clean and organized but also visually interesting. The images in the grid also change, which is satisfying to watch, though I do wish you could click on the images to see the article that goes with them.

Bad Design

NEWS10 ABC

I think this website is really disorganized/ cluttered and having black as the background color makes it feel very dark and overwhelming. I hate how enormous the banner ad is at the top and it actually hides most of the content that viewers should be seeing first. I also think the dropdowns in the navigation menu are really ugly. Also, all of the story names are so bright/stark from the black and so close together that it hurts my eyes to try to read them.
